Welcome to Sonomo Bookstore!

At Sonomo Bookstore, we believe in the transformative power of literature. Located in the heart of Dehiwala, Sri Lanka, our quaint book haven has been a haven for writers since our inception. We are delighted to welcome you to our digital world as we begin a new chapter in our journey.

Discover the story behind Sonomo Bookstore, where a passion for literature meets a commitment to providing a haven for book lovers. Founded with love and dedication, Sonomo is not just a bookstore; It is a cultural hub, a place where imaginations fly, and stories come to life.

Our humble beginnings date back to 1985, when Tammika, a true lover of the written word, decided to share her love of books with the community. The bookstore, which started as a small-scale bookstore, has now blossomed into a cherished destination for those looking for literary treasures.

At Sonomo Bookshop, we stock a wide variety of books, from timeless classics to contemporary gems. Our shelves are decked out in a variety of styles to ensure that every reader finds something that will capture their hearts and minds.

As we embrace the digital age, our commitment to fostering a love of reading remains unwavering. This website marks the first step in our digital transformation journey, bringing the essence of Sonomo Bookshop to the online world. Explore the pages, discover our curated selection, and let the magic of storytelling unfold before your eyes.

Thank you for being a part of our story. Join us in celebrating the written word and embark on a literary adventure at Sonomo Bookstore.

Happy reading!
